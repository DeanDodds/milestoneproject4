The Brewery

Inspired by the brewing scene on the West Coast of America, Adam Whilam founded North Yeast Brewing in 2019.

Adam has brewed over fifty different kinds of beer and drank a lot more, he's most comfortable in his wellies up to his knees in hops and malt. Adams favorite type of beer is a West Coast IPA.

We host BrewTours everyday which include an introduction to the brewing process by the in house Master Brewer, a brief history of the Brewery and the opportunity to sample four of our beers.

So bring colleagues, friends, family, stag/hen parties, beer geeks or who ever you want, as long as they are 18 years or older. Our tours begin 1:00pm and at 2:30pm and last approximately an hour. After the tour you are more than welcome to hang out in The Taproom if you wish.

Our Mission

Only the best spring water

Here at North Yeast Brewing it is our mission to make the finest Craft Beer in the world. We Believe to do this You need the finest and freshest ingredients. Beer is only made up of four main ingredients Water, Grains, yeast and hops , This means everyone of them is important!

Fresh Organic Grains

We source all our grains and hops from local farms and businesses. this means that they are still as fresh as possible when we use them in our beer

Aromatic Hops

When you visit the brewery the smell on brew day is fantastic. the smell of beer as it mashes and boils; it's sweet and malty from the grains, and then fresh and spicy from the hops. You must come experience it for yourself
